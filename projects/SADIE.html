SADIE-65

The Sulfuric Acid Dispersion and Injection Engine, or SADIE, is an aircraft designed for the 2024 AIAA undergraduate design challenge RFP for Stratospheric Payload Dispersion. Originally we were going to call it the Stratospheric Environmental Xperimentation, but after further consideration we instead opted to name it after a girl I liked in middle school. The documentation includes a concept proposal and the final report.
