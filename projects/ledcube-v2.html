4x4x4 LED Cube (v2)

This cube is actually not complete as of this moment. I started the project near the start of the twelfth grade at the end of 2020. My skills had improved massively since the previous year, so I felt like I was ready to do a much better job. I had also acquired my very first 3D printer, so I was able to 3D print the layer supports for soldering, making the process exponentially easier. I only completed 3 out of the 4 layers, due to busy workloads and this project not necessarily being a priority. I do plan on coming back to this at some point in the near future and completing it. I also want to build a full enclosure with a plexiglass frame and 3D printed base.
